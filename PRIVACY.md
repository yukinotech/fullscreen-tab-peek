# Privacy Policy

Fullscreen Tab Peek does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension reads the current Chrome window's tab titles and tab IDs only to render the fullscreen tab switcher inside your browser. This information stays local to Chrome and is not sent to any server.

The extension stores these preferences with Chrome extension storage:

- Whether the overlay is enabled.
- Whether the overlay appears from the left or right edge.
- The width of the edge hover trigger.

Fullscreen Tab Peek does not include analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or remote code.
