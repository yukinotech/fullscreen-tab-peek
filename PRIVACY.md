# Privacy Policy

Fullscreen Tab Peek does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension reads the current Chrome window's tab titles, tab IDs, and favicons only to render the fullscreen tab switcher inside your browser. This information stays local to Chrome and is not sent to any server.

The extension stores these preferences with Chrome extension storage:

- Whether the overlay is enabled.
- Whether the overlay appears from the top, left, or right edge.
- The viewport percentage used for the edge hover trigger.
- The drawer width or height chosen by resizing the drawer edge.

Fullscreen Tab Peek does not include analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or remote code.
